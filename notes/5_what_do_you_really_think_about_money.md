# Chapter 5 – What Do You Really Think About Money?

## Chapter Summary
This chapter explores the psychological dimension of wealth. Schäfer argues that financial results are largely determined by subconscious beliefs about money. By identifying, examining, and consciously reshaping these beliefs, individuals can remove internal barriers to wealth creation. Financial success begins with cognitive restructuring.

---

## Paragraph 5.1 – How Much Cash Do You Carry?
### Summary
Your everyday behavior toward money reflects your deeper beliefs about it.

### Key Ideas
- Small habits reveal subconscious attitudes.  
- Avoidance of money signals discomfort.  
- Familiarity builds confidence.

### Practical Strategies
- Observe your daily money behavior.  
- Increase comfort handling and discussing money.  
- Normalize financial awareness.

### Notes / Observations
- Behavior exposes belief systems.
